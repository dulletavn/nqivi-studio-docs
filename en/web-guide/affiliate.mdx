The **Affiliate Manager** allows you to share nQivi Studio with your community and earn recurring commissions when they purchase credits or subscription plans.

---

## 📈 How It Works

1. **Share your Link:** Copy your unique referral URL from the **Affiliate Manager** tab on the website.
2. **Refer Users:** When users register using your link, they are marked as your referrals.
3. **Earn Commissions:** Receive a percentage of all purchases made by your referred users.

---

## 💰 Commission Tiers

Commissions are split into performance-based levels. As your total referral sales volume grows, your profit share rate increases:

| Tier / Sales Level | Total Sales Threshold | Commission Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | $0 - $100 | **15%** |
| **Level 2** | $101 - $5,000 | **20%** |
| **Level 3** | $5,001 - $10,000 | **25%** |
| **Level 4** | $10,001 - $1,000,000 | **30%** |
| **Level 5** | Over $1,000,000 | **40%** |

---

## 💸 Withdrawals & Transactions

* **Minimum Payout:** You can request a payout once your earnings reach the minimum threshold of **$10.00 USD** (or equivalent in domestic currency).
* **Tracking Transactions:** The **Transactions** submenu displays real-time referral payments, processing payouts, and historically completed transfers.
* **Marketing Materials:** Check the **Marketing** section for logos, banners, and ready-to-share templates.
