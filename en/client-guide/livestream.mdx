The **Livestream** tab is a multi-stream live broadcasting manager. It allows you to loop pre-recorded video files and broadcast them to up to **5 platforms simultaneously** (using RTMP stream keys) without needing external encoder software like OBS.

---

## 📡 Configuring Stream Slots

You can manage up to **5 independent stream setups**. For each stream slot, configure the following:

### 1. Platform & RTMP Settings
* **Platform:** Select from YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, TikTok, or Custom RTMP.
* **RTMP URL:** The target ingest server URL (automatically populated for standard platforms).
* **Stream Key:** Paste your private stream key obtained from your platform creator dashboard.
* **YouTube OAuth Channel:** (YouTube only) Link your authenticated YouTube account to fetch metadata, schedule events, and start streams directly.

### 2. Video Source
* **Upload Video(s):** Select one or multiple video files to stream.
* **Or enter URL(s):** Provide direct URLs to hosted video streams or files (one per line).
* **Shuffle order:** Shuffle the playing sequence of the loaded videos.

### 3. Loop Mode
* **Once:** Play the playlist once and stop the stream.
* **Repeat forever:** Keep looping the videos infinitely (ideal for 24/7 streams).
* **Repeat N times:** Stop the broadcast after looping the playlist exactly N times.

### 4. Encoding Parameters
Optimize stream health to prevent lag or buffering:
* **Resolution:** Set to *Source* (keep original) or downscale (1920x1080, 1280x720) to save bandwidth.
* **FPS:** Keep original or lock to 24, 30, or 60 FPS.
* **Video Bitrate:** Configure transmission speeds (choices include 3000k, 4500k, 6000k).
* **Encoder Preset:** Adjust encoding CPU priority (choices include ultra-fast, very-fast, medium).

---

## 🕐 Scheduling Broadcasts

You can launch a livestream instantly or schedule it to start automatically in the future:
* **Quick start:** Select *start in 5, 10, or 30 minutes* from the quick dropdown.
* **Custom delay:** Input a specific number of minutes to delay the start.
* **Exact Time:** Use the calendar date-time picker to set an exact start timestamp.

Click **Schedule** to place the task into the background queue. It will update to `🕐 Scheduled` status and automatically trigger when the target time is reached.

---

## 🎨 Real-Time Graphic Overlays

A unique feature of the nQivi Livestream engine is the ability to apply or change graphic overlays **while the stream is live**.

### ✏️ Text Overlay
Chose a font, enter your text, set size/color, and adjust its position.
* **Opacity:** Make text transparent.
* **Moving text:** Toggle moving text, set pixel speed, direction, and loop.

### 🖼️ Media Overlay
Overlay an image, logo, or watermark (e.g. transparent PNG) on top of the live video.
* Adjust scale (percentage of screen), opacity, rotation angle, and moving animation paths.

> Click **Apply Overlay** to inject the graphics dynamically into the live output.

---

## 🚦 Stream Control Center

Use the global controls at the top of the tab:
* **Add Stream:** Expand a new stream configuration slot.
* **Start All / Stop All:** Start or stop all configured streams in one click.
* **Status Badge:** Monitor status colors (⚪ `Idle`, 🕐 `Scheduled`, 🟡 `Starting`, 🔴 `LIVE`, ⏸️ `Paused`, ⬛ `Stopped`, ❌ `Error`).
* **Refresh Status:** Force update stream health stats (otherwise updates automatically every 10 seconds).
