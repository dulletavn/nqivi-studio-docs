## Overview

**nQivi Studio** is an all-in-one automation ecosystem designed to simplify high-frequency media production. By blending advanced Artificial Intelligence (LLMs, TTS, Image/Video generators) with local high-performance rendering engines, nQivi Studio enables content creators, marketers, and teams to build, edit, translate, and stream videos at scale.

nQivi Studio consists of two main parts:
1. **The Web Portal (studio.nqivi.com):** A centralized dashboard to manage your account, purchase rendering credits, generate API Keys, add sub-accounts, and view billing details.
2. **The Client Desktop App:** A local Gradio-powered application that executes high-performance rendering tasks (video editing, audio styling, transitions, subtitling, and multi-streaming) directly on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and zero latency.
